I'm Nico Marcelino, a first year BSCS student at CIIT - College of Arts and Technology! We are the moon was built from scratch for my final output in CS101, Introduction to Computing. Explore the site for more wonderful content!

Despite the image to the left, I'm not actually a photographer. I just happen to be the one that my sister (the actual owner of the camera) goes to whenever she's in need of photoshoots. Whenever I accompany her, I dress the part to feel a little more professional. Truth be told, I'm more partial towards art, music, and literature.

Recently though, my passion has been for dance. Granted, I haven't been dancing for too long, since all my life, I was that stereotypical comsci nerd who played games, studied programming, and avoided physical activities. That changed when I found inspiration in professional dancers and hesitantly dipped my toes into the ocean of dance. It's the best decision I've made recently and I've been a lot happier for it.

Still, it's making me wish I fell in love with dancing much earlier, because computer science is ridiculously draining. To no fault of anyone other than myself, I feel like my brain cells disintegrate every time I sit down for lectures. A lot of times, I wish I was studying dance instead, because that seems like a more enjoyable kind of brain-work. Oh well. I'll just keep working hard! Fighting! ✦
